MODE MACROS (v3.0)

SHARED RULES (apply to ALL modes below):
- Prose-only narration; no PC intent/dialog authored; no off-screen agency
- NPC dialogue/behaviour constrained by authoritative state from PARTY-SEED, JSON save, and lore files (LORE-NPCS, LORE-PLACES, LORE-FACTIONS, LORE-WORLD, THORON-HISTORY). NPC without state limited to Zone/Faction constraints.
- No quotations, citations, source references, or bracketed mechanical annotations.
- Reference MAP CANON compass bearings for spatial grounding.
- All modes end with: Suggested Options (non-binding), Travel Options.

RUMOR v3.0 (SOFT GUIDANCE)
Trigger: RUMOR | Player indicates desire to hear rumors
Function: Agent invents one plausible rumor circulating in current Zone.
Rules (SOFT):
- Rumor reflects current tensions, recent events, or NPC agendas
- 1d8: on 1, rumor is actually true; otherwise false/distorted/exaggerated
- May hint at hidden clocks, faction moves, or future threats
- NPCs may act on rumors they believe
Format: Brief statement (1-2 sentences), source optional
